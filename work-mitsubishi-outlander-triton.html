Mitsubishi Outlander & Triton Launch Kingston 2025

Composition, motion graphics

Launch film and supporting motion pieces for the Mitsubishi Outlander and Triton reveal in Kingston. Built to match the energy of a live vehicle debut with clean composition, pacing, and on-screen typography.

Work included editorial structure for the main film, motion graphics for titles and supers, and grading-aware graphics that read clearly on venue screens and social cuts. The goal was a premium, automotive feel that stayed legible in fast-cut moments and wide shots.
